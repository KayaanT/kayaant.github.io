WHO WE ARE

Taifa Engineering Ltd. is a Canadian Consulting and Engineering Services Company comprised of Professional Engineers and Certified Engineering Technologists specialized in power system engineering. Our team is adept in all aspects of power system engineering in power generation, transmission, distribution and renewable energy programs. Our international portfolio of past projects includes clients located in Canada, United States, Cuba, Chile, Trinidad, Oman, Grand Cayman Islands, Madagascar, and Tajikistan.









OUR PROMISE

Our objective is to provide expert engineering design with the utmost attention to safety, energy efficiency, and reliability. We guarantee to provide excellent client satisfaction by delivering high-quality, professional engineering expertise while ensuring cost-effective delivery.
